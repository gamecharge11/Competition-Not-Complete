Covid Care

Being Physically Healthy

Keep yourself healthy during the pandemic
👉🏽 Drink lots of water
👉🏽 Keep yourself healthy and fit by doing basic exercise ( i.e Yoga )
👉🏽 Add more garlic / ginger to your diet
👉🏽 Intake of steam is MUST!!
👉🏽 Wash re-usable with slightly hot water.
👉🏽 Take in ' zinc ' and ' vitamin-C' tablets as immunity boosters



Being Mentally Healthy

👉🏽 Listen to music at home at a pleasant volume. Look for board games to entertain children, tell stories and future plans.
👉🏽 Do ring up your friends and relatives and talk ,but not about Covid , you have so many topics to talk, be cheerful be positive !!
👉🏽 Your positive mood will help protect your immune system, while negative thoughts , let's say -are not gonna help you in any way.
👉🏽 If you find your self in a situation where you have Covid symptoms , then consult a doc , please do not surf on the internet.
👉🏽 Maintain discipline in the home by washing your hands, and put up a sign or alarm for everyone in the house ( as well as your neighbors / colleagues etc )
👉🏽 Avoid sending fatalistic messages. Some people don't have the same mental strength as you (Instead of helping, you could activate pathologies such as depression).
👉🏽 Most importantly, firmly believe that this shall also pass and we will be safe...!!
